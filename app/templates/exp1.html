Know where your recommendations come from!

Explore AudioBook categories

AudioRama's database consists of 110,000 audiobooks. These audiobooks span 23 different categories and 176 sub-categories. To explore the distribution of categories, please click the button below.

Explore Narrators

The database consists of audiobooks narrations from 26,698 distinct narrators and 45,919 distinct authors. This author-narrator ratio (2:1) can be attributed to multiple narrators contributing to books in multiple categories, as you will see later on in this page.To explore the distribution of narrators, please click the button below.

Explore Narrator-Category Combinations

The database consists of 54,963 distinct narrators-category pairs. Some narrators are popular in multiple categories, an example is Joel Leslie, who is a popular narrator in categories ranging from romance to mysteries. To explore the distribution of top narrator-category pairs, please click the button below.

Explore Category Clusters

One of the initial steps in building our recommendation engine is to convert a representative book vector (with relevant key words) into a high dimensional embedding using a pre-trained Glove model. Please refer to the about section for more details. We can look at key dimensions of books in different categories by a dimensionality reduction method called uniform manifold approximation and projection or UMAP. To explore different category clusters, please click the button below. Notice how local order is preserved within all but one category.

Explore Narrator Clusters

Similarly, we can explore clusters of books written by top narrators. In these clusters, you will not observe local order as one narrator can contribute to multiple different genres/categories of books. However, there are some exceptions, like the pink cluster that corresponds to Dave Wright. Dave Wright is a popular non-fiction narrator and most of the books in this particular cluster are books related to health and wellness, hence the local order.
